Background

I'm Naman Mehra

An HCI Designer & Creative Technologist based in London, with an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction from UCA. I work at the intersection of design and engineering — where interfaces feel alive.

I believe the best digital experiences are the ones that disappear — when the tool gets out of the way and the human just flows.

What I Do

Design + Code + Motion

I design interfaces grounded in research, then build them with code. Animation isn't decoration — it's communication. Every transition, every micro-interaction carries meaning.

Philosophy

Less Text, More Feel

If a section can be understood without a label, remove the label. Let layout, spacing, and motion do the talking. Apple-level restraint — smooth, purposeful, inevitable.
